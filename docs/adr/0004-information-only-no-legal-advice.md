# Position the product as information-only: no legal advice, not affiliated with USCIS

The product is strictly an informational self-help tool — it pre-fills forms and sends reminders but never gives legal advice, recommends an eligibility category as counsel, or implies USCIS/government affiliation — to stay clear of unauthorized practice of law (UPL), state immigration-consultant ("notario") statutes, and App Store Guideline 5.2.3. Every filing is gated behind explicit "informational only / not legal advice / not affiliated with USCIS" consent, and counsel must validate the disclaimers, Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and the form-preparer posture before public launch.

## Consequences

This boundary governs copy, feature scope, the preparer-section handling, and onboarding consent across the entire product. It is a constraint invisible in the code yet load-bearing on every user-facing surface.
